📊 ESDA Primer: Land Use & Demographics (2012–2021)

ESDA is just EDA with spatial context — every data point has a location, and the “where” matters. You can do it in Excel or go deeper with Python libraries like GeoPandas.

This primer explores how land use and demographics across Vietnam are connected. Using spatial autocorrelation techniques like Moran’s I and LISA, we uncover patterns that reveal how different types of land use (agriculture, forestry, homesteads) relate to factors like income, population, and employment.
